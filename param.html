Pet return policy

Normally, when people adopt pets they usually get together really well and do not seem to have any problem.
Although, having said that a meagre amount of people sometimes find them in a situation
where the pet they adopted is not a good match for them, in that case
one can give them back to shelter using the number below.

Some imperative information

If one is bringing a new pet in their house, they need to take care of certain things as listed below

  • One needs to be ready with all necessary things such as leash, brush, harnesh, pet carpet etc.
  • All harmful chemicals such as phenyl, insecticides, other cleaning chemicals.
  • Other important things you deem necessary.
